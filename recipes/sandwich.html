Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A sandwich is a popular food featuring fillings like meat, cheese, vegetables, or spreads placed between slices of bread, acting as a portable meal or snack, with variations ranging from simple cold cuts to elaborate hot creations, known globally as a versatile dish

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 30g salted butter
  • ⅓ cup vintage cheddar cheese or Gruyère
  • 30g mozzarella, freshly grated

Steps

  1. Generously spread butter on both sides of each slice of bread.
  2. Heat a pan over medium-low. Toast each slice for about 1 minute per side — just to lightly crisp.
  3. Flip one slice, pile on the cheddar and mozzarella, then top with the second slice.
  4. Press gently with a spatula. Cook for about 3 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Flip the sandwich and cook for another 3 minutes, pressing gently again, until perfectly golden and the cheese inside is fully melted.
  6. Slice in half. Watch that glorious cheese pull happen. Eat immediately.
