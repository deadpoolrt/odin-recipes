BLT Sandwich

Description

B = Bread, L = Lettuce, T = Tomato

Ingredients

  • Bread ( 2 )
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Acquire Bread and toast if preferred
  2. Acquire lettuce and place in between bread
  3. Acquire Tomato, slice and place in between bread
  4. Voila! YOU GOT A BLT OIDWHOIJDOWIH
